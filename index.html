About Us

Vikas

A studious boy with an ambition in his heart to prove his mettle, to win over the world, took pause for a second to meet the girl of his dreams, to get married and be a team to take on the world. Administrative attitude, yet jovial, sincere son, an awesome friend, a promising business analyst and now will be a loving and caring husband; he is our very own – Vikas!

Monika

An intelligent girl, with shyness in her beautiful eyes, paved her way graciously wherever she went and eventually into the heart of our friend. Intelligence, talent, beauty with a tinge of shyness marks the grace of this wonderful girl, she is all set to mark her presence in the new role of a wonderful wife, daughter in law and of course bhabhi; she is our very own – Monika!
